Program Overview

SAFE STOP is a scenario-based, real-world video training program designed to educate drivers on lawful behavior during traffic stops.

The program uses actual encounter scenarios narrated by experienced law enforcement professionals to improve compliance, reduce confusion, and prevent unnecessary escalation.

It is designed for learner’s permit applicants, new licensees, and license renewals.

Step 1: Participant Information

The participant enters basic information, including a self-reported license or permit number.

This allows the training to be associated with the correct individual without requiring DMV system access or database verification.

Step 2: Video Training Modules

Participants complete short, scenario-based video modules built around real-world traffic stop encounters.

Modules are narrated by experienced law enforcement and explain:

  • lawful expectations during a stop
  • how to communicate clearly
  • what behaviors can escalate a situation
  • how to respond safely and appropriately

Step 3: Completion Confirmation

After completing the training, the participant receives proof of completion.

Confirmation may include:

  • email or text confirmation
  • a certificate ID number
  • a verifiable completion record

This confirmation can be presented during a licensing or renewal process.

Step 4: Verification

Completion can be confirmed through a simple verification process using a certificate ID or participant information.

No DMV integration is required, reducing implementation barriers while maintaining accountability.
