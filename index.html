SAFE STOP CALIFORNIA

A Driver Interaction Education Program (DIEP)

Scenario-based, real-world video training designed to help drivers safely and lawfully navigate traffic stops, reduce escalation, and lower costs for both the public and the state.

Education • Confidence • Safety
5–10 minutes Short scenario-based learning designed to fit into the licensing process.
Verifiable — DMV Completion can be shown to DMV staff or checked through a verification workflow.
No DMV data access License or permit numbers are self-reported only. No DMV database integration or validation is required.

For DMV & Public Safety Leadership

SAFE STOP CALIFORNIA is a lightweight, scalable education solution designed to reduce escalation during routine traffic stops while minimizing implementation burden on state agencies.

Public Safety Impact

  • Reduces escalation during traffic stops
  • Improves driver compliance and understanding
  • Supports officer safety and communication
  • Provides consistent, statewide education

Cost & Efficiency Benefits

  • Potential reduction in arrests and citations
  • Lower court and administrative workload
  • Reduced tow, impound, and detention costs
  • Minimal implementation cost

Implementation Advantages

  • No DMV system integration required
  • No access to DMV data or databases
  • Works with existing licensing workflows
  • Fast pilot deployment capability

Scalable Model

  • Applies to permits, new licenses, and renewals
  • Compatible with driver education providers
  • Centralized platform supports multi-state rollout
  • Expandable to national implementation

Impact & Cost Reduction Potential

California processes millions of driver license transactions annually. Even a small reduction in traffic stop escalations can translate into meaningful cost savings for both drivers and the state.

Millions Annual permit, license, and renewal transactions
1–2% Reduction Potential decrease in escalation-related incidents
High Impact Reduced court, enforcement, and administrative costs
Potential savings areas include:
  • Reduced arrests and citations from avoidable escalation
  • Lower court processing and legal system burden
  • Fewer vehicle tow and impound events
  • Reduced insurance impact on drivers
